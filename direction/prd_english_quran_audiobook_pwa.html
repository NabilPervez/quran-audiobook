# Product Requirements Document: English Quran Audiobook PWA ## 1. Project Overview A Progressive Web App (PWA) that provides a high-quality, English-translated audio experience of the Quran. The interface and user experience should be heavily inspired by Spotify—intuitive, dark-themed, and focused on seamless audio playback and discovery. ## 2. Target Audience - English-speaking Muslims looking for a modern way to listen to the Quran. - Students of knowledge and individuals interested in understanding the Quranic message in English. - Commuters and multi-taskers who prefer audio content. ## 3. Key Features - **High-Quality Audio Playback:** Smooth streaming with standard controls (Play/Pause, Skip, Seek, Speed Control). - **Surah & Juz Discovery:** Browse the Quran by Surah (Chapter) or Juz (Section). - **Spotify-Inspired UI:** Dark mode by default, large card-based layouts, and a persistent "Now Playing" bar. - **Search & Filter:** Quickly find specific Surahs or verses. - **Personalization:** "Your Library" for saved Surahs, history, and favorite reciters/translators. - **Offline Support (PWA):** Ability to cache audio for offline listening. - **Responsive Design:** Optimized for Desktop, Tablet, and Mobile. ## 4. Proposed Sitemap & User Flow 1. **Home Screen:** Featured Surahs, "Recently Played," and "Recommended for You" sections. 2. **Browse/Search Screen:** Categorized navigation (Makkah/Madinah Surahs, Themes) and a powerful search bar. 3. **Library Screen:** User's saved Surahs, playlists, and listening history. 4. **Player View (Full Screen):** Detailed view with verse-by-verse English text synchronization (if available), playback settings, and sharing options. ## 5. Design Direction (Spotify Aesthetic) - **Color Palette:** Deep blacks, dark grays, and a vibrant primary accent color (e.g., Emerald Green or Gold). - **Typography:** Bold, clean sans-serif fonts (like Circular or Gotham alternatives). - **Components:** Rounded corners, hover effects on cards, and a sleek bottom navigation/player bar.
